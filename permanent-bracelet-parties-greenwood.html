What happens at a permanent bracelet party

Each guest chooses a chain (and any charms they like), we measure it to their wrist, ankle or neck, and join the two ends with a quick, painless micro-weld instead of a clasp. The weld is a tiny spark — it doesn't touch the skin and you feel nothing. Most fittings take just a few minutes per person, so even a good-sized group moves quickly.
