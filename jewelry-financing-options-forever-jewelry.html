Two kinds of financing, simply put

Most jewelry financing falls into one of two categories:

  • Lease-to-own (Acima, Snap, Progressive): the provider buys the item, and you make scheduled payments while using it. You own it once it's paid off — or sooner with an early-purchase option — and you can usually end the lease at any time. These programs are designed to work for a wide range of credit situations.
  • Credit (Synchrony): a credit account, often with a promotional interest-free period, generally suited to shoppers with established credit.

Approval, payments and fees are set by each provider based on the merchandise and your application. Always review the provider's specific terms before you sign.
