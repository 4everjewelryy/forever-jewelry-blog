Different watches, different care

Standard quartz

The quick, inexpensive case — a few minutes and you're done. Typically about $10–$50.

Water-resistant

These must be carefully resealed and pressure-checked after a battery change so they keep their water resistance — worth doing right.

Solar & luxury

Solar watches rarely need batteries, but when service is required it's specialized. Luxury brands use specific batteries and gaskets, so they cost a bit more.

Multiple watches
